Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat

  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl

  3. Once Butter has melted, add cream and eggs

  4. Using a spatula, pull the eggs from the edge of the center every 20 seconds

  5. When The eggs are still moist, remove from heat
